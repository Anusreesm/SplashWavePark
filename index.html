About SplashWave Park

Welcome to SplashWave Park, your ultimate destination for aquatic fun and relaxation! Established in 1999 by Anusree, SplashWave Park has grown to become a premier water theme park with multiple locations. Nestled amidst lush greenery and shimmering pools, our park offers an unparalleled escape from the ordinary, where every moment is filled with joy and excitement.

At SplashWave Park, we pride ourselves on providing a one-of-a-kind experience that brings families and friends together to create cherished memories. Whether you're seeking thrilling rides, serene wave pools, or simply a place to unwind and relax, our park has something for everyone.

Our commitment to safety, cleanliness, and customer satisfaction ensures that your visit to SplashWave Park is nothing short of extraordinary. Join us for an unforgettable aquatic adventure, where the mood is always joyful and the memories are everlasting!
